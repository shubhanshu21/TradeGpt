*   **Improvement:** Implement **Cumulative Volume Delta (CVD)** and **Order Book Imbalance (OBI)**. This prevents the model from being distracted by "Fake" technical signals.

## 4. Strategic Roadmaps

### Phase 1: The "Sovereign Edge" (Short-Term)
1.  **Dynamic Scaling (Local Z-Scores):** Transition from Global Mean to a rolling 120-minute window mean. This eliminates the "Lean Drop" visual artifacts and improves trend following.
2.  **TurboQuant INT8 Training:** Optimize the Hydra-Block kernels for 8-bit integer math to reduce epoch time to <5 hours on CPU.
3.  **Bootstrap Finetuning:** Auto-ingest the last 24 hours of data every midnight to keep the weights fresh.

### Phase 2: The "Deep-Predator" Evolution (Mid-Term)
*Target: Cross 55.0% Validation Accuracy (Institutional Mastery)*

1.  **Dynamic Local Scaling (DLS):**
    *   **The Logic:** Transition from Global `KATScaler` to a **Forward-Relative Window Scaler**.
    *   **Implementation:** Every input sequence is standardized against its own 120-minute local mean/std.
    *   **Impact:** Completely eliminates "Mean-Reversion Pull" and allows the model to trade at any price level (e.g., $10k or $100k) with equal precision.

2.  **Sovereign Microstructure Suite (Market Mood):**
    *   **Cumulative Volume Delta (CVD):** Injects a new feature tracking the net difference between market buyer and seller aggression.
    *   **Order Book Imbalance (OBI):** Analyzes the volume ratio of Bids vs. Asks in the Top 10 levels of the book. 
    *   **Volatility Signatures:** A local, zero-latency "Fear Index" derived from price tick velocity.

3.  **Architecture "Brain Surgeon" Upgrades:**
    *   **Multi-Head Latent Attention (MLA):** Adopting the DeepSeek-V3 block structure to reduce memory usage by 90% while increasing context awareness.
    *   **GRPO Router Optimization:** Rewriting the MoE router logic to penalize "Conservative" experts and reward "Extreme Accuracy" experts.
    *   **Quantization-Aware Training (QAT):** Running the training loop in 8-bit precision to double the epoch speed on your CPU.
